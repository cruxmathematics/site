Dan-Andrei Geba

Dan-Andrei Geba is a professor of Mathematics at the University of Rochester in Rochester, NY. He has experience teaching mathematics at all levels (i.e., K-12, undergraduate, and graduate), as well as supervising undergraduate honors and doctoral theses. He is the winner of the 2011 Goergen award conferred by University of Rochester for excellence in undergraduate teaching. He has been the coach of the University of Rochester math team for the William Lowell Putnam competition, which won an honorable mention in 2008 and was ranked seven times in the top twenty (out of more than four hundred participating teams) for the past ten years.
