Matthew Creek

Matthew Creek is a professor of Mathematics at Assumption University in Worcester, MA. He teaches and has taught courses in undergraduate mathematics at all levels -- from remedial courses at the University of Montana to upper-division courses at the University of Chicago -- and also teaches undergraduate courses in computer science. He was the coach of the University of Chicago's team at the International Mathematics Competition in Blagoevgrad, Bulgaria in 2015 and 2016, the latter of which came in fourth place out of seventy-two teams. He currently runs the Assumption University Problem Solving Group, which is a venture to tutor and train students in preparation for mathematical competitions.
